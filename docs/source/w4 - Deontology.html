Deontology (duty)

Deontology focuses on the inherent rightness or wrongness of actions based on duties, not just on the outcomes or consequences.

Definition

  1. Deontic 是一个希腊词，意思是duty, obligation 责任

  2. 如果某个行为违反了基本义务（例如欺骗、杀人），就算他可能导致了好结果，它依然可能是不对的。

  3. Duty / Rules can be highly specific

    • Keep promises

    • Don’t lie

    • Be fair

  4. Rule is general: apply to many circumstances

  5. Rule is basic: cannot be simpler

  6. 我们通过学习，反思来理解duty: Don’t hurt your sister

  7. 与Utilitarianism的区别：Utilitarianism says there is a single duty—to maximise utility. But Deontology says there are multiple basic duties.

WD Ross’s 7 basic duties

[!NOTE]

Ross duties are prima facie duties, 是可以根据实际情况被推翻的.

Fidelity
忠诚		 The duty to keep promises, agreements, and contracts
Reparation
赔偿		 The duty to compensate others when we have harmed them or violated their rights
Gratitude
感恩		 The duty to acknowledge and repay kindnesses and benefits received from others
Justice The duty to distribute benefits and burdens fairly, according to principles of fairness and impartiality
Beneficence
行善积德		 The duty to promote the well-being and happiness of others, including helping others in need.
Self-Improvement The duty to improve one’s own abilities and character.
Non-maleficence
不伤害别人		 The duty to avoid harming others or causing them unnecessary suffering.

Prima Facie: Rules may conflict

Ross’s list of duties are prima facie duties，它表示这些义务在“默认”情况下我们应该遵守，但在遇到冲突时 we use our own judgement, one rule may override another.

Non-hierarchical: 这些duty没有严格层级关系(non-hierarchical)（e.g., Reparation 和 Justice没有谁更重要）。But how to determine which is more important: no hard answer,use our own judgement.

[!TIP]

“I promised to visit my friend… but I see a child drowning… duty to the child overrides the promise.”“我答应去看望我的朋友……但我看到一个孩子溺水……对孩子的责任凌驾于承诺之上。

Kantian Deontology

Definition

Right = “a good will”

Good will = 出于对道德义务的尊重而行动，而非基于欲望、后果、利益或情绪。

有时候人可能会抱着善意做错事，

所以即使结果不好，只要你是出于“duty”的动机去行动，你仍然是道德的。

Categorical Imperative 绝对命令 / absolute rule

Unconditional commands of reason that apply to all rational beings universally, regardless of circumstances.

适用于所有理性生物的无条件理性命令……无论利益或情况如何

Formula of Universal Law

  • We act on ethical maxims 行为准则 or rules/principles of action
  • Moral Law: If our maxims apply to others, they apply to us too
  • 设想如果所有人都这么做，如果会导致矛盾，那么改行为就是不道德的。
  • ‘Do not cheat people’
    • 不道德，不是因为说谎会伤人，是因为当说谎这个行为被普遍化时无法不产生矛盾。
  • They have autonomy

Formula of Humanity

“Act in such a way that you treat humanity, whether in your own person or in the person of any other, never merely as a means to an end, but always at the same time as an end”

以尊重人性 end-in-themseleves 为目的，而非把人当作纯粹的工具merely means来使用。

Ends, means, and mere means

  • Treat as end-in-herself / himself
  • Never treat as merely means - “You use me!”

[!TIP]

雇一个水管工修理下水道，对方自愿 → 你把她当作手段 mean，但也尊重她作为一个人 end-in-herself。

强迫水管工劳动还拒绝支付报酬 → 把她当作仅仅的工具 mere mean，违反道德法则。

[!TIP]

如果一个杀人犯问你谁谁谁藏在哪里，你要告诉他吗？

Lying -> you treat murderer as mere means -> wrong!

You should never lying.

Dignity

people have equal dignity and worth 每个人都有平等的尊严和价值 -> 我们要平等地对待所有人

人不可替代，不可被“牺牲掉”以达成某种“更好”的系统目标

  • e.g. 偏见忽视了平等的权利和尊重

Autonomy

Autonomy: ability to think for ourselves, plan our lives, act on our values

  • 我们要尊重每个人制定自己生活计划的权利
  • 不能强迫、欺骗、替别人决定“什么对他好”
  • 即使是弱势群体，也有决定自己命运的权利

To respect autonomy, we need people’s consent.

  • e.g., collecting private data of users -> let them know!

对deontology的批评

  1. 崇拜规则 worship rules -> Morality is made for man!

    • 为什么要“无条件地遵守某些规则”？
    • 不能说谎，即使能救人？
    • 道德是为人设的，不是人为道德而活。

  2. Kant is too absolute -> No rule/duty is sacrosanct!

    • Kant: 说谎、杀无辜、违反承诺等行为绝不允许，即使能带来更好后果。
    • 面对杀人犯，你还不能撒谎保护无辜？

  3. Ross judgment no absolute algorithm.

    • 不知道如何优先排序：
      • 救人重要？
      • 守信重要？
      • 道歉重要？
      • 诚实重要？
    • 理论上强调规则，却在“冲突时”无法提供确定操作方式。

  4. 现实世界中的可操作性差

    1. 义务论对 AI、医疗、政策决策中的复杂情况缺乏操作性建议 Lack of operational advice
    2. 而Utilitarianism提供计算后果的方法，即使有争议，至少提供了量化工具 Quantitative Tools
