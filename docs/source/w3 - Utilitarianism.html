Utilitarianism 功利主义

Utilitarianism is a consequentialist ethical theory that holds that morally right actions are those that treat everyone’s happiness equally, and produce the greatest overall happiness or utility

Consequentialism

  1. Consequentialism 包含了 Utilitarianism

  2. Teleological 目的论: ends (good outcomes) determines the right

  3. Impartiality 非偏私性: your own, mine, everyones.

“Utilitarianism says… others’ interests should matter to me as well, not just my own… all interests matter equally.”

我自己的痛苦，和你、他、陌生人、动物的痛苦，只要是“程度一样的痛苦”，就必须平等看待。

所以，utilitarianism 具有非自我中心、全球性、甚至跨物种的伦理视野。

History

Bentham

Utilitarianism 起源于 Enlightenment (启蒙时代)，挑战了Religion 宗教。

  • 功利主义是理性主义的产物，强调通过“推理和衡量”来解决伦理问题，而非盲从传统和权威。

  • 它在历史上曾是“进步”、“解放”的代名词，如Women’s emancipation, anti-

    slavery

“Greatest happiness of the greatest number of people”

我们不能只追求“让最多人幸福”，而要追求maxiunm overall happiness。

享乐主义 Hedonistic utilitarianism

John Stuart Mill

创造了principle of utility

What ought I do?

Consequences = utility, Calculate and aggregate consequences • Right = produce best possible consequences (utility)
• Wrong = not doing so
• POU: Maximise utility

Uitility

  • Utility: value to individuals
  • This value = interests
  • Interests = harms and benefits
  • Benefits (goods) = positive utility
  • Harms (bads) = negative utility
  • Maximise utility = sum up benefits and harms

要考虑的因素

[!NOTE]

Utilitarianism主张：凡是能感受痛苦与快乐的生物（包括动物）都应被平等计入道德考量. 同时，furture, likelihood, weight, duration, magnitude 也应该被考虑。

Longtermism 即使是一百年后的后代，只要他们能体验快乐或痛苦，就应该平等地纳入我们今天的决策中。

Hedonistic & Preference Utilitarianism（享乐主义 vs 偏好主义）

Hedonistic utilitarianism: Bentham

[!NOTE]

Pain is pain, happy is happy. All happy are equal.

“Two masters: pleasure & pain” “Pushpin is as good as poetry” 图钉和诗歌一样好 -> happy level all same “Pain is pain and pleasure is pleasure”

Pleasure and pain 是人类行为的根本动因 → 所以功利主义要关注这两个量。

不区分“高级”或“低级”的快乐：只看intensity和duration，快乐就是快乐，痛苦就是痛苦。

Hedonistic utilitarianism: Mill

[!NOTE]

Pleasure level: 区分了高级快乐和低级快乐 (阅读 vs 玩游戏，艺术 vs 喝酒).

“Better to be a dissatisfied Socrates than a satisfied fool.” “宁做不满足的苏格拉底，也不做满足的傻瓜。

Pleasure level not equal, have higher and lower pressures. (e.g., Study is better than playing)

Preference Utilitarianism 偏好满足

[!NOTE]

preference satisfied > happiness at this moment.

E.g., 即使成为 computer scientist 的过程很辛苦痛苦，只要你真心希望如此，最终达成了，它就产生了正效用。

  • Happiness != pleasure
  • Happiness = preference satisfaction
  • Utility = preferences
  • Positive utility = satisfied preferences 实现了
  • Negative utility = unsatisfied preferences 没实现

Pleasure Machine vs. Experience Machine

Pleasure Machine（快乐机器）- 你可以一生都待在一个永远让你感到快乐的机器中。

Experience Machine（体验机器）- 你“感觉”自己实现了偏好（比如当了科学家）

  • hedonistic utilitarian: good, 只要快乐就好
  • Preference utilitarian: bad，因为preference是关于真实实现，不是虚幻感受。

Act Utilitarianism vs. Rule Utilitarianism

[!NOTE]

Act utilitarianism (AU) calculates utility for each individual action, while rule utilitarianism (RU) follows general rules that tend to maximize utility in the long run.

Ru better with ordinary intuitions

如果杀一个人能救五个人，act认为可以杀（如果net utility最大）。

杀死无辜的人违反了促进最佳后果的rule（“dont kill people”）

-> RU 更符合普通的道德直觉 ordinary intuitions (AU 可能会允许杀人！)
